### ğŸ§­ Step 1 â€“ Go to Dashboard

---

### ğŸ§­ Step 2 â€“ Open Settings

---

### ğŸ§­ Step 3 â€“ Select â€œWhitelist IPâ€

![3](https://files.catbox.moe/o7y7xk.jpg)

### ğŸ§­ Step 4 â€“ Enter Your IP Address

![4](https://files.catbox.moe/j5rj89.jpg)

---

### ğŸ§­ Step 5 â€“ Click "Add to Whitelist"

> âœ… _IP successfully added to whitelist_
